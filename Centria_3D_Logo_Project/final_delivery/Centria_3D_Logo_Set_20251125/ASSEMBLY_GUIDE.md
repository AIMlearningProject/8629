# Hardware Assembly Guide
## Centria Logo Branding Items

### Pin Assembly

**Required Hardware:**
- 10mm butterfly clutch pin backs
- Super glue (cyanoacrylate) or epoxy

**Steps:**
1. Test fit pin back in cylindrical mount
2. Apply small amount of glue to mount
3. Press pin back firmly
4. Let cure for 24 hours before use
5. Test: Pin should not wobble

**Sources for Hardware:**
- Amazon: "10mm butterfly clutch pin backs"
- AliExpress: Bulk pin backs
- Local craft store

---

### Magnet Assembly

**Required Hardware:**
- 12mm diameter × 2mm thick neodymium magnets
- Optional: Super glue for permanent mount

**Steps:**
1. Clean magnet recess
2. Test fit magnet (should be snug)
3. Press magnet into recess
4. Optional: Add tiny drop of glue for permanent mount
5. Test: Magnet should hold 500g on steel surface

**Magnet Specifications:**
- Size: 12mm × 2mm
- Material: N42 or N52 neodymium
- Coating: Nickel plated
- Pull force: >2kg

**Safety Note:** Keep magnets away from:
- Pacemakers and medical devices
- Credit cards and magnetic strips
- Electronic devices
- Small children (choking hazard)

---

### Keyring Assembly

**Required Hardware:**
- Standard keyring split rings (25-30mm)
- Optional: Jump rings for easier attachment

**Steps:**
1. Thread split ring through loop
2. Spiral ring around loop opening
3. Test: Pull with 5kg force - should hold
4. Optional: Use jump ring for easier key attachment

**Hardware Sources:**
- Hardware stores
- Amazon: "split ring key chains"
- Bulk: AliExpress

---

### Cake Mould Preparation

**No assembly required, but important prep:**

**Before First Use:**
1. Wash thoroughly with warm soapy water
2. Rinse completely
3. Dry fully
4. Optional: Light food-safe oil coating

**For Each Use:**
1. Grease mould lightly
2. Press into dough (5mm thick recommended)
3. Remove gently
4. Clean immediately after use

**Material Safety:**
- Must use food-safe materials only
- PETG: Check manufacturer certification
- Resin: Must be food-safe cured resin
- DO NOT use regular PLA for food contact

**Cleaning:**
- Hand wash only
- Warm soapy water
- Dry completely
- Do not dishwasher

## Batch Production Tips

### For 50+ Units

**Pins:**
- Use jig for consistent glue placement
- Let cure in groups of 10
- Quality check each one

**Magnets:**
- Order magnets in bulk
- Use push jig for consistent seating
- Check polarity (all same direction!)

**Keyrings:**
- Pre-open split rings with tool
- Assembly line setup speeds process
- Test random samples for quality

**Moulds:**
- No assembly needed
- Package with usage card
- Include food-safety certification
